Phase 5: Research & Innovation

In this final phase, you'll dive into cutting-edge AI advancements. You'll experiment with emerging models, contribute to open-source projects, and even write your own papers or blogs. This is where creativity meets code.

Topics to Explore

  • Reading and interpreting AI research papers (arXiv, NeurIPS, ICLR)
  • Fine-tuning large language models (LLMs)
  • Experimenting with Vision Transformers, Diffusion Models, etc.
  • Contributing to open-source AI libraries
  • Publishing your work: blogs, GitHub repos, and Kaggle notebooks

Recommended Resources

  • Recent AI Papers on arXiv
  • Hugging Face Blog
  • Papers With Code
  • OpenAI Research

Project Ideas

  • Train a chatbot using your own dataset
  • Write a blog explaining a recent breakthrough (e.g., Mixture of Experts, SFT, or DPO)
  • Contribute to Hugging Face Transformers or LangChain
  • Use Gradio/Streamlit to demo your models
